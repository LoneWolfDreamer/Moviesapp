Black Widow

Genre: Action, Superhero

Duration: 134 minutes

Director: Cate Shortland

Synopsis: Black Widow is a Marvel Studios' action-packed spy thriller. The film follows the journey of Natasha Romanoff, a.k.a. Black Widow, as an agent and assassin of the Red Room organization before she joined the Avengers. The film confronts the darker parts of her ledger when a dangerous conspiracy with ties to her past arises. The film stars Scarlett Johansson as Natasha Romanoff.
