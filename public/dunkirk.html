Dunkirk

Genre: Historical War Thriller

Duration: 106 minutes

Director: Christopher Nolan

Synopsis: Dunkirk is a movie that chronicles the evacuation of British soldiers who got trapped in the harbor and on the beaches of Dunkirk, France, in late May and early June of 1940. The movie is set during the Battle of France in World War II.
