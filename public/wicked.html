Wicked

Genre: Musical Fantasy

Duration: 160 minutes

Director: Jon M. Chu

Synopsis: The Wicked movie, set to be released in 2024, is a vivid reimagining of the classic The Wizard of Oz. It focuses on the untold stories of Oz's most famous characters, particularly the Wicked Witch of the West and her unlikely friend, Glinda the Good Witch. The plot covers Elphaba's studies at Shiz University and her path to ultimately becoming the Wicked Witch of the West, alongside her unlikely friendship with Glinda.
