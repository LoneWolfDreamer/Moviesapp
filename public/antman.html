Ant-Man

Genre: Superhero

Duration: 117 minutes

Director: Peyton Reed

Synopsis: Ant-Man is a superhero movie about a con artist named Scott Lang who gets a shot at redemption when his mentor, Dr. Hank Pym, asks him to protect a super-powered suit that can shrink him down to minuscule size. Lang fights off villains seeking to steal the invention and organizes a heist that could save the world.
