Venom: Let There Be Carnage

Genre: Superhero

Duration: 112 minutes

Director: Ruben Fleischer

Synopsis: The movie "Venom" follows the story of Eddie Brock, a struggling journalist who becomes the host of an alien symbiote. This merging gives him superhuman strength and power, and he becomes the antihero known as Venom. The film revolves around his battle against the notorious founder of the Life Foundation, Carlton Drake.
