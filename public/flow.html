Venom: Let There Be Carnage

Genre: Adventure film

Duration: 84 minutes

Director: Gints Zilbalodis

Synopsis: Set in a post-apocalyptic world, the film follows a black cat navigating a landscape where water levels are dramatically rising. With no dialogue, the story unfolds through the cat's journey of survival, encountering other animals like a Labrador, a capybara, and a secretary bird. Together, they face natural dangers and explore submerged cities, emphasizing themes of resilience, cooperation, and the beauty of nature.
