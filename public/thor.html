Thor

Genre: Superhero

Duration: 114 minutes

Director: Kenneth Branagh

Synopsis: Thor, a powerful but arrogant warrior god, is cast down to Earth by his father Odin as punishment for reigniting an ancient war against the frost giants. While on Earth, he falls in love with scientist Jane Foster, which teaches him valuable lessons about humility and responsibility. As dark forces from his homeland threaten Earth, Thor must confront these challenges and prove himself worthy of his powers.
