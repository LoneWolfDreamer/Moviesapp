Rampage

Genre: Science Fiction Monster

Duration: 107 minutes

Director: Brad Peyton

Synopsis: Rampage is a movie about a primatologist named Davis Okoye who shares an unshakable bond with George, an albino western lowland gorilla who has been in his care since birth. A rogue genetic experiment gone awry transforms George into a raging monster, and he teams up with Davis to stop two other mutated animals from destroying Chicago.
