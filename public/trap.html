Trap

Genre: Psychological Thriller

Duration: 105 minutes

Director: M. Night Shyamalan

Synopsis: The movie follows a serial killer evading a police blockade while attending a concert with his daughter. The story revolves around Cooper taking his daughter, Riley, to see a pop artist perform live, only to discover that the concert is a trap to catch him.
