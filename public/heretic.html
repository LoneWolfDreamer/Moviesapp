Venom: Let There Be Carnage

Genre: Horror-Thriller film

Duration: 111 minutes

Director: Scott Beck and Bryan Woods

Synopsis: The film follows two missionaries of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who attempt to convert a reclusive Englishman, only to realize that he is much more dangerous than he seems. The movie delves into the fragile intersection of faith and fear, exploring how personal beliefs can be tested in the face of the unexplainable. It promises to take its audience on a variety of dark twists and turns.
